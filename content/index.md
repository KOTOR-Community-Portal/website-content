# Welcome!

Welcome to the KOTOR Community Portal! This website is dedicated to the iconic _Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic_ video games. Our goal is to be a one-stop destination for gameplay tips, technical support and mod installations, ensuring that everyone can continue to play these classics with full function into the present day. Whether you intend to utilize one of our mod compilations or create a mod set of your own, we'll be happy to work with you to help you through every step of the process to get your install bug-free and mods set up just how you want them.

You can also join us on the official [KOTOR Discord](https://discord.gg/kotor#rules)! Our community is happy to answer general questions, provide build advice, work with you to construct stable mod setups, or just chat with fellow fans.
